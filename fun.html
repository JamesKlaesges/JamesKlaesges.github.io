Our Family Pet: Bullet the Bull Snake

Allow me to introduce "Bullet", otherwise known as "Snake" or "Snaky".

He (at least I think it's a he, nobody knows for sure) enjoys eating mice and taking the occasional soak in his tub.

One of the nice things about Bullet is that he requires very little care. All he needs is to be feed and his cage cleaned once a week. In addition, over the winter, Bullet enters a state of semi-hibernation, where he lowers his body temperature and doesn't eat or do anything for more than 3 months.
